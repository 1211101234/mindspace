      Grounding Techniques

      5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
      1. Name 5 things you can see
      2. Name 4 things you can touch
      3. Name 3 things you can hear
      4. Name 2 things you can smell
      5. Name 1 thing you can taste
      Cold Water Reset
      Splash cold water on your face — it activates your body's dive reflex and slows your heart rate almost immediately.
      Body Scan
      Sit quietly. Starting from your toes, slowly notice each part of your body moving upward. Just observe — no judgment. Takes 2–3 minutes.
