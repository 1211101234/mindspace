Safety & Guidelines

Last updated April 2025 · Applies to all Mindspace rooms and features

If you are in immediate danger, please call emergency services now: 999 (Malaysia) or 112 (International). This page and this app cannot substitute for emergency response.

What Mindspace is

Mindspace is an anonymous, peer support chat space. It is a place to talk, listen, and feel less alone. It is not a medical service, a therapy platform, or a crisis intervention system.

Sage is an AI companion — not a therapist, not a counsellor, and not a substitute for professional mental health care. Sage is designed to support, not to diagnose or treat.

🌿 About Sage (AI)

Sage uses a large language model (Claude by Anthropic) to generate responses. It can make mistakes, miss nuance, and is not trained as a clinical tool. Responses from Sage should never be treated as medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a professional or a crisis line below.

Community guidelines

  • Be kind. Everyone here is going through something. Treat others the way you'd want to be treated on your hardest day.
  • No harassment, targeting, or hate. Content that targets individuals, groups, or identities based on who they are will be removed.
  • No sharing of harmful methods. Do not describe or share methods of self-harm or suicide. Discussions of struggle are welcome — detailed how-to content is not.
  • No spam, promotional content, or unsolicited URLs. This is a support space, not an advertising platform.
  • Anonymity is mutual. Do not try to identify, dox, or expose other users. Everyone here is anonymous by choice.
  • You can leave anytime. There is no obligation to stay in a conversation that doesn't feel safe.

Moderation

Mindspace has volunteer moderators who help maintain a safe environment. Moderators can remove messages, enable slow mode on a room, and review flagged content. Moderators cannot see your IP address, private messages, or any identifying information.

You can report any message using the ⚑ button next to it. Reports are reviewed by moderators and are anonymous.

Privacy

  • No accounts, no registration, no email addresses.
  • Your anonymous persona is generated randomly on each session and discarded when you leave.
  • Room messages are stored temporarily (up to 80 messages per room) to provide history for people who join mid-conversation. Messages are automatically pruned.
  • Private journal entries (if used) are encrypted in your browser and never sent to our servers.
  • We do not sell data. We do not show ads.
  • Crisis events (detected distress signals) are logged without personal identifiers for safety monitoring purposes only.

Contact

For safety concerns, abuse reports, or moderation issues that cannot be handled in-app, you can reach the operator at the contact listed in the platform where you accessed this app.

To apply as a volunteer moderator, visit /mod/apply or use the in-app application form.

Mindspace is not a medical device, does not provide clinical care, and is not regulated by any healthcare authority. Use of this platform is at your own discretion. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.
